Questions & Answers

How do we get started?

Reach out for an estimate and we'll come take a look at your yard and give you a quote. You can also use the Request an Estimate tab at the top of this page to submit your info online.

Are you insured and licensed?

We are fully insured with general liability and workers compensation insurance. We are licensed to do business in Redding and surrounding areas. We focus on jobs less than $500 and do not currently hold a contractors license.

What are your specialties?

Smaller jobs and ongoing maintenance. We love partnering with property management companies and making rental properties look great. We also do excellent one-day clean-ups.

How do I leave feedback?

Click the Leave Feedback tab at the top of this page, select your name and service visit, give us a rating, and submit — it goes directly into our system.

Do you work with property managers?

Absolutely — it's one of our specialties. We coordinate directly with property managers to keep rental properties looking great year-round.
